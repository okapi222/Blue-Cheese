Grounded in COM-B

Not Training. A Behavior-Change Engine at Scale.

Our program is built on the COM-B model ‚Äî an evidence-based framework that shows behavior change requires all three conditions to be met simultaneously.

Capability + Opportunity + Motivation = Behavior Change

Capability ‚Äî Knowledge & Skills

Foundation training and demo sessions build the AI skills and knowledge PMs need to work differently.

Opportunity ‚Äî Environment & Support

Clinics, labs, and community create the environment and social support to practice and experiment.

Motivation ‚Äî Drive & Confidence

Leadership sponsorship, peer demos, and commitment moments drive the will to change.

Based on the COM-B model (Michie, van Stralen & West, 2011)
